Breakfast All Day In a Big Way!

Heritage brick walls, huge plate glass windows and high ceilings hung with Modernicas' Big Bubble lamps house a hip and happening crowd on most days of the week at the eclectic Blue Fox Cafe. Situated on Antique Row in the heart of the Harris Green neighbourhood, the Fox has been serving generous portions of fresh, local, comfort food to a loyal clientele since 1991.

Perennial favourites include Eggs Benny (ten different types and reportedly The Best in the City!), giant gourmet beef burgers, hand cut fries, moroccan chicken & house-roasted savory pork quesadillas!

Emphasis is placed on fresh, locally sourced products - coffee is organic; chicken is free run. Breakfast is served all day with good music, good value, good cheer... and of course, a little west coast funk!
